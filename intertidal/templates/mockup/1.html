Intertidal

My work comes to you from the intertidal zones of Singapore, Hong Kong, and Vancouver. From the human and nonhuman voices that exist in relation with beaches, ports, harbours, swamps, wetlands, and dykes. These are space of flux, ecological richness, nationalist and capitalist infrastructure, complex histories, and environmental devastation. Each of these sites, whether island, continent edge, or archipelago shares the common histories of imperialism, development, land reclamation, and dispossession. By reading and listening intently to these spaces, I seek to represent and reimagine these spaces in decolonial ways.
